How to Play

Your goal is to score points by finding new words using 42 tumbles.

Click and drag the letters up or down to tumble them and find new words.

Each letter you shift costs one of your 42 tumbles. You can keep counting words until you run out of tumbles. You can tumble more than one letter at once!

For example, changing word to worm will cost you 10 tumbles because m is 10 letters from d.

Changing worm to warm will cost you 12 tumbles because a is 12 letters from o.
